About

Andrea is a Mexican-American multidisciplinary artist from the Bay Area. Her main focus was traditional fine arts such as painting and illustration, up until 2022, when she began practicing digital media arts such as 3d modeling, animation, digital illustration, and more. She never limits herself to one medium, but rather explores each one and often finds ways to combine them.
